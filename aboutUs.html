Get To Know Us

Touchdown Aviation

Set in a beautiful location in Noida, Touchdown Aviation is an academy with class. There are two partners, Capt Rajat Kaura and Niti Kaura.

Capt Rajat Kaura is an experienced pilot with over 3 decades of flying experience in 5 countries spanning 3 continents. He has taught ground subjects for over 6 years in India and Australia. He has a variety of experience in both the defence and commercial spaces. Having started his own career at a young age, he has been in your shoes and understands your perspective.

Niti Kaura is an accredited counsellor, with a background in Psychology. She is experienced in dealing with high pressure situations and clients, and has worked intensively with suicidal and depressed clients. She is empathetic, and understands the pressures of being a pilot, and is familiar with the Aviation Industry, making her the ideal point of contact for you.

Capt Rajat Kaura

As your instructor, Capt Rajat Kaura brings a wealth of experience and passion to Touchdown Aviation. With flying and instructing experience, he knows his stuff, and he knows how to teach his stuff.

He is a Post-Graduate in Defence and Strategic Studies from DSSC, Wellington, and a graduate from the National Defence Academy. He has flown for over 16 years with the Indian Air Force, and another 17 years as a commercial pilot in India, Australia, Bangladesh, Dubai and Germany.

His qualifications include ME-CIR, NVG, CPL(H), ATPL(H) and UAV licence. Don't worry if these are just a string of letters to you right now, soon you'll know exactly what they mean! But if you're in a rush to know, you can always reach out to us.

He has saved more than 400 lives as a rescue pilot, and has been awarded gold wings in Australia. As an instructor, he groomed cadets at the prestigious National Defence Academy.
