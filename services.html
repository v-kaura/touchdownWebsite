What We Offer

Core Academic Ground Subjects

Here at Touchdown Aviation, we are your one stop shop for all your aviation requirements, including ground subjects. We understand that each student's needs are unique, so we offer you the flexibility of choosing the subjects you want to do. You can pick and choose subjects, or do them all.

We offer the following ground subjects:

  • Navigation
  • Meteorology
  • Aircraft Technical
  • Air Regulations
The courses we offer are not just for Indian pilot's licences. We cater to those with domestic and international ambitions, offering Indian/US/Australian Aeroplane/Helicopter pilot's licences.

Our courses are 12-14 weeks long. We align our courses with the DGCA exams so that you can take the exam just after the end of the course.

We are committed to assist you in your studies. Our system of weekly tests and feedback lets you (and us) know what your strengths and weaknesses are.

Flight Training

As for flying, we collaborate with AFT Center in California, USA.

The flying course is 8 - 10 months in duration, offering you 250 hours of flying with single-engine, multi-engine, and instrument rating endorsements. On successful completion of the course and the Skill Tests for Flying, you will be eligible for both DGCA and FAA licences.

Radio Telephony and Medicals

In addition to your ground and flying subjects, to become a pilot you will also need FRTOL and Class-I Medicals. We will assist you in obtaining both of these.

Additional Services

Additional services include

  • Free career guidance and counselling, to help ensure that you have the attitude and mindset to succeed.
  • Free guidance for DGCA related applications like computer number, application for exams, medicals, etc.
  • Free guidance for FAA and CASA, so that a foreign land feels as close as home.
