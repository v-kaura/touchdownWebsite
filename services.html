What We Offer

Core Academic Course Subjects

Here at Touchdown Aviation, we are your one stop shop for all your ground subjects. We understand that each student's needs are unique, so we offer you the flexibility of choosing the subjects you want to do. You can pick and choose subjects, or do all the subjects.

We offer the following subjects:

  • Navigation
  • Meteorology
  • Aircraft Technical
  • Air Regulations
The courses we offer are not just for Indian pilot's licences. We cater to those with domestic and international ambitions, offering Indian/Australian Aeroplane/Helicopter pilot's licences.

Course Structure

Our courses are structured to provide you with constant feedback so you can always stay on top of your education. Weekly exams give you opportunity to test yourself regularly so you can maintain your strengths while targeting your weaknesses.

Services

Services include

  • Free career guidance and counselling, to help ensure that you have the attitude and mindset to succeed.
  • Free guidance for CASA, so that a foreign land feels as close as home.
  • Free guidance for DGCA related applications like computer number, application for exams, medicals, etc.
