Welcome to Touchdown Aviation!

Flying, the career for you!

At Touchdown Aviation, we believe in a holistic learning experience. You are unique, and so is your learning style. We leverage this to provide the ideal learning environment for you to build yourself to be the best pilot you can.

Our courses are structured to provide you with constant feedback so you can always stay on top of your education. Weekly exams give you opportunity to test yourself regularly so you can maintain your strengths while targeting your weaknesses.

International Opportunities

With a global perspective, comes a global ambition. Our goal is to prepare you not only for the immediate domestic environment in which you may fly, but also for the international skies! Our ground classes are specifically designed to address the DGCA requirements for flying in India as well as FAA (Federal Aviation Administration) in USA, and CASA (Civil Aviation Safety Authority) in Australia, giving you the confidence to excel no matter where you are.

Flying High

Our students are our pride. Nothing makes us more proud than watching them thrive and achieve their goals. Our one of a kind teaching engages students as we incorporate techniques to advance their critical thinking skills. An exam is held every week to challenge their understanding and track their learning. By embedding seriousness and drive they are evermore encouraged to push their limits and achieve their dreams.
